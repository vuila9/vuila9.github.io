Many mini-tools
Description

This page is a collection of various small-scale projects or tools inspired by everyday ideas and experiences. For instance, while browsing social media, I might come across something interesting that I want to replicate. Whenever a cool idea comes to mind, I make a note of it and plan to code it later. Once completed, I upload it here, with the most recent projects displayed at the top. Click the expand button to explore each one. Think of this page as a fun space to experiment with mini tools, which may or may not be useful to you—though some are designed for my personal use.
